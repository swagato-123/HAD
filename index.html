Human Fall Detection

Fall detection devices automatically employ the technology to detect and get fast assistance for a senior that is prone to falls. The system can detect the elderly's falling by acceleration analysis. Then it will get the elderly's geographic position and send fall alarm short message to caregivers. So the elderly who has fallen can get timely help to minimize the negative influence.
